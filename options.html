Note!

The "Tab Audio Behaviour" option requires additional permissions to function, specifically the "Tabs" permission. Due to a quirk in Chromium, this permission is defined as "Read your browsing history". This permission does NOT give the extension permission to view your actual browsing history, but allows it to see what tabs you currently have open, which we use ONLY to check to see if any tabs are playing audio. If you are okay with that, press "Allow" on the next dialogue.

For more info, refer to the Chrome Developer Docs.
